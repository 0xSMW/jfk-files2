Amended Operational Approval for Overseas Deployment
B. Turbeville, John E. Howard, Operational Approval, Amendment, Overseas Operations, United States
The memorandum amends the subject's operational approval to authorize his use outside the United States based on evaluated information and CSI authority.
Dated 6 January 1971 and signed by John E. Howard, the memorandum revises prior approval for subject AMMUG/1 by authorizing his operational use outside the United States in line with his PRQ Part II dated 13 February 1965 and a memorandum request from 31 December 1970, granted under the authority of CSI and CSI-F 10-5.
